About This Program

The Creator Fund Program is a community-driven initiative aimed at supporting rising content creators by helping them access tools, guidance, and potential sponsorships. This is not affiliated with YouTube, TikTok, or any other platform — it’s a private initiative to help talented individuals grow.

Submissions are reviewed manually, and selected participants may be contacted for future opportunities. By applying, you consent to be contacted for relevant updates.
